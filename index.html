繁體中文

摘要

GridFix 不收集、不儲存、也不傳輸您的任何個人資料。所有照片處理皆在您的裝置上完成。

我們收集哪些資料

無。GridFix：

  • 不需要註冊帳號
  • 不收集姓名、電子郵件或任何個人識別資訊
  • 不使用任何分析、追蹤或廣告 SDK
  • 不會將您的照片或任何資料上傳至任何伺服器

照片存取

GridFix 僅在您主動選取照片時，透過系統的照片選取器讀取該張照片；儲存校正結果時，會請求「僅新增」的相簿權限，將照片存回您的相簿。照片的讀取、編輯與儲存全部在您的裝置上進行，不會離開您的裝置。

App 內購買

訂閱與買斷購買由 Apple 的 App Store（StoreKit）處理。我們無法取得您的付款資訊。相關資料處理請參閱 Apple 的隱私權政策。

本機儲存的資料

GridFix 僅在您的裝置上儲存 app 偏好設定（例如放大鏡位置）與免費版每日輸出次數。這些資料不會離開您的裝置，刪除 app 即一併移除。

兒童隱私

GridFix 不收集任何人的資料，包括兒童。

政策變更

若本政策有變更，我們會更新本頁面並修改「最後更新」日期。

聯絡方式

如有任何疑問，請來信：info@gridfixapp.com

English

Summary

GridFix does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. All photo processing happens entirely on your device.

What data we collect

None. GridFix:

  • Requires no account or registration
  • Does not collect your name, email, or any personally identifiable information
  • Contains no analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs
  • Never uploads your photos or any other data to any server

Photo access

GridFix reads a photo only when you actively select it through the system photo picker. When saving a corrected photo, it requests add-only access to save the result back to your photo library. All reading, editing, and saving happens on your device; your photos never leave it.

In-app purchases

Subscriptions and the lifetime purchase are processed by Apple’s App Store (StoreKit). We never receive your payment information. See Apple’s Privacy Policy for how Apple handles this data.

Data stored locally

GridFix stores only app preferences (such as loupe position) and the free tier’s daily export count, on your device. This data never leaves your device and is removed when you delete the app.

Children’s privacy

GridFix does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the “Last updated” date.
